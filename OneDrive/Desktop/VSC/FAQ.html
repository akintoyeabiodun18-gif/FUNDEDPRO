Frequently Asked Questions

How are withdrawals requested? ▼
You can initiate a withdrawal request directly from your trader dashboard. Simply navigate to the "Payout" section, verify your closed profit, select your preferred payment method (Crypto or Bank Transfer), and confirm the request. Ensure your account is fully KYC verified to avoid any processing delays.
Which platforms can I trade on? ▼
Currently, our platform grants access to Ctrader, Tradelocker, MatchTrader, Volumetrica, and MT5. Please note that MT5 and Ctrader are not available to clients located in the USA. All other platforms (Tradelocker, MatchTrader, and Volumetrica) are available to US traders.
How long does it take to receive a reward? ▼
Our payout process is completed within 24-48 business hours. As part of our commitment to fast service, if you do not receive your payout within 2 business days, we will add a $500 bonus to your payout.
Is news trading allowed? ▼
Yes, news trading is fully permitted on all FUNDEDPRO accounts. You are free to trade during high-impact news events without any restrictions or lot size limitations.
Are weekend holdings allowed? ▼
Yes, you are permitted to hold your positions open over the weekend on all account types. We do not force you to close your trades at the market close on Friday.
